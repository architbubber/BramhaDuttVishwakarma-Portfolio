Postdoc

Two types of fellowships for postdoctoral researchers

We are eagerly looking for postdoctoral researchers interetsed in interdisciplinary projects concerning climate change, Geodesy, and hydrology. The institute offers two types of fellowships: IOE and CV Raman. For both these fellowships, the candidate must get in touch with a faculty member at the Institute, prepare a short research proposal and secure consent letter from the prospective advisor. Then the candidate should apply through the IISc portal: https://iisc.ac.in/post-docs/

If you are interested in working with us, please get in touch with Dr.-Ing. Bramha Dutt Vishwakarma at bramha@iisc.ac.in

Internship and research projects

We are always looking forward to working with young and bright Masters or Bachelors students. If you are interested in doing an internship or Masters research theisis with us, please prepare a cover letter describing your motivation, research direction, and a potential research project. Send the cover letter and your CV to bramha@iisc.ac.in

Please note that there is no fellowship available from our side. If you have identified a fellowship that you want to apply for and need our support, then please write to us with ample time.
